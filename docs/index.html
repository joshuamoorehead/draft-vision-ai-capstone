Why Draft Vision AI?

Draft Vision AI blends cutting-edge machine learning with a rich, interactive web experience to revolutionize how you analyze and simulate the NFL draft. Whether you’re a fan, analyst, or team scout, our platform delivers data-driven predictions, hands-on mock drafts, and a vibrant community—all in one place.

Unlock every stage of the draft process:

  • Ensemble & Neural Network Predictions — Multiple models trained on decades of draft data forecast each prospect’s likely draft slot.
  • Custom Mock Draft Simulator — Choose your draft year, number of rounds, and participating teams, then pick in real time (with trade proposals!).
  • Dual Analytics Dashboards — The Player List shows current NFL stars; the Big Board previews next year’s class, both filterable by team, position, and more.
  • Head-to-Head Comparison — Compare any two players side by side across stats, physical measurables, and performance metrics.
  • Create-a-Player & Pro Comparison — Input custom stats to see your player’s predicted draft position and their closest professional analog.
  • Interactive Community Hub — Publish, browse, and comment on public mock drafts; view draft grades and receive in-depth AI-generated analysis.
  • Secure & Responsive — User authentication, role-based access, and dynamic visualizations ensure a seamless experience on any device.
